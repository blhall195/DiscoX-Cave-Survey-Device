Firmware Update

Keep your DiscoX up to date

Download the latest firmware and follow the simple steps below to update your device.

📋

How to Update

Updating your DiscoX firmware is quick and easy. Follow these steps:

  1. On your DiscoX, navigate to "Update Firmware" in the device menu and select it.
  2. Connect your DiscoX to your PC using a USB-C cable. A new drive will appear in your file explorer.
  3. Download the firmware file using the button on this page.
  4. Drag and drop the downloaded firmware.uf2 file into the explorer window that appeared.
  5. Your DiscoX will automatically reboot with the new firmware once the file has finished copying.

That's it — your device is now running the latest firmware.
