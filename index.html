Built for Cavers

Everything you need, even a disco mode...

Designed from the ground up for wet, muddy, and unforgiving cave environments — with repairability and simplicity at its core.

💧

Water Resistant Design

A completely sealed polycarbonate & PETG case with an internal epoxy coating protects electronics from water. Great for surveying wet passages.

🔧

Fully Repairable

Every component can be made, sourced, salvaged, 3D printed, or laser cut. No proprietary lock-in — just fix it and get back to surveying.

📲

Bluetooth Paperless Survey

Connects wirelessly to TopoDroid and SexyTopo. Take readings and send them straight to your phone — zero paper, fewer errors.

🎯

Onboard Calibration

An intuitive calibration algorithm runs entirely on the device — no second device needed.

⚠️

Anomaly Detection

Readings are automatically checked for magnetic interference from helmet lights, watches, or bolts. Get alerted before bad data enters your survey.

🟢

RGB Status LED

A colour-coded LED band shows device state at a glance — red for stabilising, green for reading taken, purple for leg detected. Plus a dedicated disco button.

📡

Extended Bluetooth Range

Long range Bluetooth delivers over 20m line of sight range, keeping you connected at a distance.

Sealed USB-C Port

A resin-capped USB-C port isolates internals while providing easy access for charging and programming settings.

⚙️

Configurable Settings

Tweak settings via the onboard menu or plug in and edit the settings.json file directly on your PC.

🔋

Reliable Battery Monitoring

A dedicated battery monitoring chip gives you accurate charge levels.

🛡️

Gorilla Glass & Quartz

Scratch-resistant Gorilla Glass display cover and quartz glass laser protector handle the scrapes and bumps of cave life.

</>

Open Source & Reprogrammable

Can be built with C++ or optionally CircuitPython. Inspect the code, modify behaviour, all on your own terms.
