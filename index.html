Built for the harshest environments

Designed for wet and muddy cave environments, the case is constructed from a mixture of tough polycarbonate and PETG, it also includes a scratch-resistant gorilla glass display cover and quarts glass laser protector.

  • See Full Specs.

Clarity in Colour - RGB status indicator

The DiscoX's RGB LED light band status indicator, alerts the user to anomalous results and indicates whether the device is being held stable before a reading is taken, improving survey accuracy. There's also a dedicated disco button to activate "Rave Mode".

  • See Full Specs.

A modular repariable design

Even if you do manage to break it - no problem, the device is completely repairable. Most of the components are available to buy from commerical vendors or can be salvaged from other DiscoX devices. Alternatively, get in touch, and I'll help fix it.

Features

The DiscoX is designed to be simple to use and integrate into all your .

Scratch Resistant protection

Made from quarts glass, the laser cover resists scratches, no need to worry about whiping away mud.

Completley waterproof

A complelty sealed case design ensures no wet components, great for surveying wet passages

Bluetooth Connectivity

The DiscoX currently supports blutooth connection to both TopoDroid V6 and SexyTopo.

Nice Buttons

Four easy to press membrane buttons on the top of the device ensure simple operation even with gloves.

Custom Settings

To modify the key settings within the device simply plug it into a PC and modify the settings file.

Compact ergomonic design

Fits in the palm of your hand, comes with a integrated wrist strap.

Intuative Calibration

An intuative onboard calibration algorithm keeps your device accurate without the need for a second device.

Anomoly detection

The discoX checks readings after you take them and reports if it suspects magnetic anomolies.

Waterproof USB-C port

A resin capped usb-c port complely isolates the electronic components from the outside of the device while allowing easy access for programming/charging.

Accurate Battery Montioring

A dedicated battery monitoring chip provides you with a reliable battery status.

Industrial Accelerometer

Designed for harsh environments this accelerometer should withstand knocks and bumps.

Large Screen

A 128x128 pixle screen big enough for you to see everything you need at the same time.
